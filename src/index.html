ABOUT METROUI

MetroUI is an open source toolkit for developing with HTML, CSS, and JS. Quickly prototype your ideas or build your entire app with responsive grid system, extensive prebuilt components, and powerful plugins. MetroUI developed to build websites in Windows MetroUI Style and include general styles, grid, layouts, typography, 100+ components and routines, 500+ built-in icons.

MetroUI is an open-source and has a free MIT license, and was a member of Microsoft BizSpark Startup.
